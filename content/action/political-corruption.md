## Objectives
{{< gridlist >}}
### Support Reform
Support campaign finance reform and anti-corruption measures. Vote for candidates who reject big money and lobbyist influence.
<--->
### Get Involved
Get involved in local politics and hold leaders accountable. Educate yourself and others about how money shapes policy.
<--->
### Demand Transparency
Demand transparency in government and corporate lobbying.
<--->
### Promote Civic Education
Promote civic education and encourage others to participate in the democratic process.

## What You Can Do
{{< gridlist >}}
### Support campaign finance reform
Back groups like RepresentUs, Common Cause, or Move to Amend that fight corruption and big-money influence in politics.
<--->
### Vote with integrity
Research candidates using tools like OpenSecrets.org or BallotReady to see who funds them before you cast your vote.
<--->
### Track lobbying influence

### Join local political action

<--->
### Educate yourself and others
Share resources like the Brennan Center for Justice reports or League of Women Voters guides on money in politics.
<--->
### Demand transparency laws
Push for local and national legislation requiring real-time disclosure of political donations and lobbying.
<--->
### Promote civic education
Volunteer with schools or groups like iCivics to build political literacy in your community.
<--->
### Hold leaders accountable
Write letters, organize petitions, or use tools like ContactingCongress.org to directly pressure representatives.
