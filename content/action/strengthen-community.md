## What You Can Do
{{< gridlist >}}
### Start or join a tool library
Use guides from Shareable or find existing ones through the Local Tools Library Directory.
<--->
### Organize swaps and mutual aid
Join Buy Nothing groups, Freecycle, or create local swap events to share skills and resources.
<--->
### Build neighborhood networks
Use platforms like Nextdoor, Meetup, or Mutual Aid Hub to connect with nearby people and projects.
<--->
### Launch or support community fridges
Contribute to the Community Fridge Project or start one locally to reduce waste and share food.
<--->
### Support independent media
Subscribe to Democracy Now!, ProPublica, or local independent outlets instead of corporate-owned media.
<--->
### Create learning spaces
Host study circles, workshops, or join peer-led learning groups like P2PU (Peer 2 Peer University).
<--->
### Celebrate local culture
Support neighborhood events, artists, and cultural organizations that keep community voices strong.
<--->
### Join cooperative projects
Participate in co-ops for housing, food, or energy. Directories are available through the U.S. Federation of Worker Cooperatives or Co-op Directory Service.
{{< /gridlist >}}
