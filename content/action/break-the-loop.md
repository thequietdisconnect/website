## Objectives

Resist reward systems and microtransactions designed to exploit you.

## What You Can Do
{{< gridlist >}}
### Learn to spot dark patterns
Explore resources like Deceptive Design (formerly Dark Patterns) to recognize manipulative tactics used in apps and websites.
<--->
### Install browser defenses
Use extensions like uBlock Origin, Privacy Badger, or DeArrow to cut out manipulative ads, autoplay, and clickbait.
<--->
### Resist reward loops
Read Natasha Dow Schüll’s Addiction by Design or Tristan Harris’s work at the Center for Humane Technology to understand how predatory reward systems are engineered.
<--->
### Block microtransactions
Turn off in-app purchases on devices (parental control settings on iOS/Android) or use tools like Freedom or Cold Turkey to block apps designed to exploit microtransactions.
<--->
### Choose ethical alternatives
Opt for open-source or ad-free platforms. Signal for messaging, ProtonMail for email, or F-Droid for community-built apps.
<--->
### Track your habits
Use tools like RescueTime, Screen Time, or Digital Wellbeing to identify which apps manipulate your time and attention.
<--->
### Educate your circle
Share videos like The Social Dilemma or guides from Humane Tech with friends and family to raise awareness.
<--->
### Support humane design
Follow and support movements like the Humane Tech movement or Ethical Design Manifesto that push back against manipulative tech.
{{< /gridlist >}}
