## Objectives
{{< gridlist >}}
### Support Workers
Support worker-owned co-ops and unions. Advocate for fair wages, benefits, and protections for gig workers.
<--->
### Challenge Insecurity
Challenge the myth of hustle culture and endless insecurity. Educate others about labor rights and precarity.
<--->
### Push for Policy Change
Push for policies that protect all workers, not just full-time employees.
<--->
### Promote Workplace Democracy
Promote workplace democracy and encourage worker participation in decision-making.
{{< /gridlist >}}
## What You Can Do
{{< gridlist >}}
### Support worker-owned co-ops
Buy from or join co-ops listed in the U.S. Federation of Worker Cooperatives or Co-operatives UK directories.
<--->
### Back unions and labor organizing
Support groups like the AFL-CIO, SEIU, or new efforts like the Amazon Labor Union and Starbucks Workers United.
<--->
### Advocate for gig worker protections
Join campaigns like Gig Workers Rising or Rideshare Drivers United pushing for fair pay and benefits.
<--->
### Challenge hustle culture
Share resources from movements like The Nap Ministry or Slow Work that resist burnout and glorified precarity.
<--->
### Educate yourself on labor rights
Use resources from the Department of Labor, ILO (International Labour Organization), or Know Your Rights campaigns.
<--->
### Push for stronger policies
Support legislation like the PRO Act (Protecting the Right to Organize) or local minimum wage and paid leave initiatives.
<--->
### Promote workplace democracy
Encourage participatory decision-making through models like sociocracy, holacracy, or by supporting Democracy at Work Institute.
<--->
### Stand in solidarity
Join worker strikes, sign petitions, or contribute to strike funds like the Emergency Workplace Organizing Committee (EWOC).
{{< /gridlist >}}
