Support advocacy groups like the NAACP Legal Defense Fund or ACLU in protecting marginalized communities from systemic injustice.
<--->
### Support reentry programs
Donate to or volunteer with groups like Defy Ventures, Center for Employment Opportunities, or Homeboy Industries, which provide training and opportunities for returning citizens.

### Challenge harsh sentencing laws
Advocate for ending mandatory minimums and three-strikes laws by engaging with campaigns like FAMM (Families Against Mandatory Minimums).
<--->
### Push for systemic policy change
Support legislative reforms such as the First Step Act in the U.S. or local initiatives to reduce prison populations and invest in community safety.
