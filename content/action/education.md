## Objectives
{{< gridlist >}}
### Learn Beyond the Classroom
Learn outside the classroom: read, explore, and ask questions. Support alternative education models and unschooling.
<--->
### Challenge the Script
Challenge standardized testing and rote memorization. Share knowledge and skills in your community.
<--->
### Advocate for Meaningful Learning
Advocate for creative, adaptive, and meaningful education.
<--->
### Share and Mentor
Share your skills, mentor others, and create opportunities for collaborative learning in your community.
{{< /gridlist >}}
## What You Can Do
{{< gridlist >}}
### Explore alternative models
Learn about Montessori, Waldorf, Sudbury schools, or unschooling networks that prioritize curiosity and self-direction.
<--->
### Use open learning platforms
Take free courses on Khan Academy, Coursera, edX, or MIT OpenCourseWare to learn outside traditional institutions.
<--->
### Join or create learning circles
Participate in peer-led groups like P2PU (Peer 2 Peer University) or local book clubs and study circles.
<--->
### Challenge the testing culture
Support organizations like FairTest that campaign against high-stakes standardized testing.
<--->
### Practice community knowledge-sharing
Host workshops, DIY skillshares, or local “teach-ins” to spread knowledge outside formal classrooms.
<--->
### Mentor and be mentored
Offer your skills on platforms like Mentor Collective, or find mentors through community programs and online networks.
<--->
### Advocate for educational justice
Support groups like Education Reimagined or Teachers for Social Justice pushing for creative, inclusive, and equitable schooling.
<--->
### Create and share open resources
Contribute to Creative Commons projects or share your own tutorials and guides freely online.
{{< /gridlist >}}
